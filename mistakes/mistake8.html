Mistake #8: Confusing Function Inputs/Outputs

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They plug in x incorrectly or misinterpret f(x) as something else.

Why it’s wrong

Incorrect substitution leads to the wrong value and answer.

What the SAT is testing

Function comprehension and careful substitution.

How to avoid it

Always rewrite f(x) explicitly and plug in the correct number.

Try it yourself!

If f(x) = 2x + 1, what is f(3)?
