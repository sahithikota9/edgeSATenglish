Mistake #3: Solving for the Wrong Variable

Section: Math

Why students pick it

The equation looks familiar, so students solve automatically without checking what the question asks.

Why it’s wrong

The SAT may ask for an expression based on the variable, not the variable itself.

What the SAT is testing

Instruction-following and precision.

How to avoid it

Write down exactly what the final answer must represent before solving.

Try it yourself!

Solve: If 2x + 3 = 7, what is 2x + 3?
