Mistake #1: Choosing the Answer That Sounds Right

Section: Reading & Writing

Why students pick it

The wording feels formal and academic, matching the tone of the passage.

Why it’s wrong

The SAT rewards precision. If an answer adds even a small idea not stated in the passage, it is incorrect.

What the SAT is testing

Evidence-based reasoning and attention to detail.

How to avoid it

Ask whether every part of the answer can be supported by a specific line in the passage.
