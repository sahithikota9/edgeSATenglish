Passage

Urban gardens provide both ecological and social benefits. They reduce air pollution, offer green spaces, and encourage community engagement. Despite limited space in cities, creative solutions like rooftop gardens have become increasingly popular.

Words in Context Tips

  • Ecological → relating to the environment.
  • Engagement → participation, involvement.
  • Rooftop → on top of a building.
  • Despite → in spite of.
  • Increasingly → more and more over time.

Tip: Look at surrounding context, identify subject and action to infer meaning of words.

Common Errors

1. Ignoring Context Clues

Use nearby words to help deduce the meaning of unfamiliar vocabulary.

2. Confusing Cause & Effect

Look for words like "despite," "because," or "therefore" to clarify relationships.

3. Misreading Tone

Determine if the passage is positive, negative, or neutral to guide answers.

4. Skipping Transition Words

Words like "despite" indicate contrast; missing them can alter meaning.

5. Overlooking Key Modifiers

Adjectives and adverbs often signal important differences in meaning.
