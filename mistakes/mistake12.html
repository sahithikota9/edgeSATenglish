Mistake #12: Not Checking for Extraneous Solutions

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They solve equations quickly without considering domain restrictions.

Why it’s wrong

Some solutions may not satisfy the original equation or constraints.

What the SAT is testing

Logical reasoning and constraint management.

How to avoid it

Always check each solution against the original problem.

Try it yourself!

Solve √(x) = -2. Does x = 4 work?
