Mistake #15: Estimation Errors

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They round too early or make rough guesses instead of careful calculation.

Why it’s wrong

Small errors compound and lead to incorrect answers.

What the SAT is testing

Accuracy and careful arithmetic.

How to avoid it

Use precise calculation for each step; only round at the final answer.
