Passage

Renewable energy sources like solar and wind are becoming more viable alternatives to fossil fuels. These technologies reduce carbon emissions and can generate jobs. Yet, intermittency and storage remain technological challenges that must be addressed.

Questions

  1. What are the benefits of renewable energy mentioned?
  2. Identify two challenges associated with renewable energy.
  3. Why is storage important in renewable energy?

Words in Context Tips

  • Viable → practical, workable.
  • Intermittency → irregular availability.
  • Carbon emissions → release of CO₂ into atmosphere.
  • Technologies → applied scientific knowledge.
  • Addressed → dealt with or solved.

Tip: Check surrounding sentences for clarifying definitions or examples.

Common Errors

Unique Mistake: Overlooking Keywords

Failing to notice words like "yet" or "must" can cause misinterpretation of contrast or emphasis.
