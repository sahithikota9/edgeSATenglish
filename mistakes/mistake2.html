Mistake #2: Overgeneralizing from One Detail

Section: Reading

Why students pick it

One sentence strongly supports the idea, so students apply it too broadly.

Why it’s wrong

The SAT penalizes answers that extend a claim beyond the passage’s scope.

What the SAT is testing

Understanding of scope and limits.

How to avoid it

Check whether the answer applies to the whole passage or just one line.
