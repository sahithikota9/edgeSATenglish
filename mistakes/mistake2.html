Mistake 2 – Common SAT English Errors

← Back to Home

1. Comma Splice

Incorrect: I went to the store, I bought milk.
Correct: I went to the store. I bought milk.
Tip: Two independent clauses cannot be joined with just a comma. Use a period or semicolon.

2. Apostrophe Errors

Incorrect: Its a beautiful day.
Correct: It's a beautiful day.
Tip: "Its" is possessive; "it's" is a contraction of "it is."

3. Run-on Sentence

Incorrect: She loves music she plays guitar.
Correct: She loves music; she plays guitar.
Tip: Break long sentences or use proper punctuation to avoid run-ons.

4. Confused Words

Incorrect: Their going to the park.
Correct: They're going to the park.
Tip: Know homophones (their/there/they’re, your/you’re) to avoid mistakes.

5. Parallelism

Incorrect: She likes hiking, to swim, and biking.
Correct: She likes hiking, swimming, and biking.
Tip: Ensure lists/phrases are grammatically parallel.
