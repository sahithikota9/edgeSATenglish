Mistake #14: Misapplying Geometry Rules

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They apply the wrong formula for area, volume, or angles.

Why it’s wrong

Incorrect formulas yield completely wrong answers.

What the SAT is testing

Geometry knowledge and correct application.

How to avoid it

Review formulas and double-check which formula fits the problem.

Try it yourself!
