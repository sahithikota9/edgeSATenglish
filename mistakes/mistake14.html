Passage

Public libraries play an important role in communities, offering free access to books, technology, and learning programs. Despite declining funding, many libraries have adapted by providing digital resources and hosting community events to stay relevant.

Questions

  1. List three services libraries provide according to the passage.
  2. What challenges do libraries face?
  3. How have libraries adapted to these challenges?

Words in Context Tips

  • Declining → decreasing, reducing.
  • Adapted → changed to fit new conditions.
  • Relevant → important or useful in current context.
  • Digital → electronic, computer-based.
  • Community → a group of people living in the same area.

Tip: Understand the context by analyzing cause and effect, contrast, and emphasis.

Common Errors

Unique Mistake: Misreading Contrast

Words like "despite" or "however" signal contrast. Failing to notice these can mislead comprehension.
