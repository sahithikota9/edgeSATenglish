Mistake #4: Misreading the Question Stem

Section: Reading & Writing

Why students pick it

They skim the question and assume what’s being asked.

Why it’s wrong

Answering the wrong question leads to a wrong choice, even if your calculation is correct.

What the SAT is testing

Careful reading and comprehension under time pressure.

How to avoid it

Underline or highlight exactly what the question asks.
