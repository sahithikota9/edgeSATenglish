Mistake 4 – Common SAT English Errors

← Back to Home

1. Wordiness

Incorrect: Due to the fact that it was raining, we stayed inside.
Correct: Because it was raining, we stayed inside.
Tip: Use concise phrasing; avoid unnecessary words.

2. Confusing Adjectives/Adverbs

Incorrect: She sings beautiful.
Correct: She sings beautifully.
Tip: Use adverbs to modify verbs, adjectives to modify nouns.

3. Incorrect Idioms

Incorrect: She is good in painting.
Correct: She is good at painting.
Tip: SAT tests idiomatic expressions; memorize common ones (good at, interested in, capable of).

4. Pronoun Clarity

Incorrect: When Susan talked to Mary, she was upset.
Correct: When Susan talked to Mary, Mary was upset.
Tip: Ensure pronouns clearly refer to the correct noun.

5. Misuse of Colons/Semicolons

Incorrect: I have three pets; a dog, a cat, and a parrot.
Correct: I have three pets: a dog, a cat, and a parrot.
Tip: Use colons before lists; semicolons to join independent clauses.
