Passage

The ecosystem of the Amazon rainforest supports countless species of flora and fauna. Deforestation and human encroachment threaten this delicate balance, leading to potential extinction of rare species. Conservation efforts are ongoing, yet the challenge remains daunting.

Words in Context Tips

  • Encroachment → gradual intrusion, often by humans.
  • Delicate → easily damaged, fragile.
  • Daunting → intimidating, difficult.
  • Conservation → protection, preservation.
  • Extinction → disappearance of species.

Tip: Use context clues from surrounding words to choose the most appropriate meaning.

Common Errors

1. Misinterpreting Cause & Effect

Pay attention to words like "leading to" or "yet" to understand relationships.

2. Confusing Vocabulary

Similar words may differ in nuance; check definitions carefully.

3. Ignoring Adjectives

Adjectives like "delicate" or "rare" provide critical context for comprehension.

4. Misreading Transition Words

Connectors signal contrast, addition, or consequence; they affect meaning.

5. Overlooking Verb Nuances

Pay attention to tense and action words; they indicate timing and cause.
