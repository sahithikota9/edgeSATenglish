Mistake #13: Skipping Steps in Multi-Step Problems

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They try to solve too quickly and omit steps.

Why it’s wrong

Skipping steps increases arithmetic errors.

What the SAT is testing

Methodical problem solving and accuracy.

How to avoid it

Write every intermediate step carefully, even if it seems obvious.
