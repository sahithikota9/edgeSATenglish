Passage

Electric cars have become increasingly popular as environmental awareness grows. While they reduce greenhouse gas emissions, challenges such as battery disposal and charging infrastructure remain. Governments are incentivizing adoption through subsidies and tax breaks.

Questions

  1. What are the benefits of electric cars mentioned in the passage?
  2. Identify two challenges associated with electric cars.
  3. What solutions are governments providing?

Words in Context Tips

  • Incentivizing → encouraging by offering benefits.
  • Adoption → act of taking up or using.
  • Infrastructure → supporting systems or structures.
  • Subsidies → financial aid from government.
  • Emissions → release of gases into the air.

Tip: Pay attention to surrounding context and cause-effect relationships.

Common Errors

Unique Mistake: Ignoring Cause & Effect

Failing to notice how one event leads to another can result in incorrect answers. Look for words like "while" and "through."
