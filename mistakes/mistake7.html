Mistake #7: Overlooking Negative Signs

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They simplify too quickly or forget a negative when moving terms across an equation.

Why it’s wrong

Sign errors can completely change the answer in SAT math problems.

What the SAT is testing

Attention to arithmetic signs and careful computation.

How to avoid it

Write every step clearly, especially when distributing negative signs.
