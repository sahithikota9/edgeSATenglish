Mistake 7 – Common SAT English Errors

← Back to Home

1. Sentence Fragments

Incorrect: Because I was tired.
Correct: I went to bed early because I was tired.
Tip: Make sure each sentence has a subject and a verb and expresses a complete thought.

2. Comma After Conjunction

Incorrect: I went to the store, and bought milk.
Correct: I went to the store and bought milk.
Tip: Do not put a comma before a conjunction unless it separates two independent clauses.

3. Wrong Pronoun Case

Incorrect: Between you and I, this is secret.
Correct: Between you and me, this is secret.
Tip: Use object pronouns after prepositions.

4. Wrong Adjective Form

Incorrect: She looks more prettier today.
Correct: She looks prettier today.
Tip: Avoid double comparative forms (more + adjective ending in -er).

5. Incorrect Parallel Construction

Incorrect: He likes to swim, biking, and runs.
Correct: He likes swimming, biking, and running.
Tip: Keep the same grammatical form in lists for clarity.
