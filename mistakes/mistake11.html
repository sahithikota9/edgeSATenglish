Passage

The advent of technology has reshaped the modern workplace. Automation has replaced many routine tasks, while collaboration tools enable remote work. However, this shift also requires employees to develop new skills and adaptability.

Words in Context Tips

  • Advent → arrival, beginning of something important.
  • Automation → use of machines to perform tasks.
  • Adaptability → ability to adjust to change.
  • Reshaped → changed, transformed.
  • Collaboration → working together to achieve a goal.

Tip: Focus on surrounding context and how words are used in sentences.

Common Errors

1. Misinterpreting Main Idea

Identify the primary concept of each paragraph; supporting details explain it.

2. Ignoring Shift Words

Words like "however," "while," "also" indicate contrast or addition.

3. Confusing Vocabulary

Ensure you understand nuanced words like advent, adaptability, automation.

4. Overlooking Modifiers

Adjectives and adverbs clarify the main idea; don’t skip them.

5. Misreading Parallel Structures

Lists like "Automation has replaced... while collaboration tools enable..." must be parallel.
