Mistake #5: Ignoring Units or Conversions

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They read numbers but ignore units (e.g., miles vs. km, hours vs. minutes).

Why it’s wrong

The SAT expects answers in the units specified. Ignoring conversions leads to errors.

What the SAT is testing

Attention to detail and careful unit conversion.

How to avoid it

Always write down units and convert if needed before calculating. Circle or underline units in word problems.

Try it yourself!

Convert 5 miles to kilometers. (1 mile ≈ 1.609 km)
