Passage

Advances in medical research have improved treatment options for chronic diseases. Early detection and preventive care are emphasized to reduce long-term complications. Collaboration between hospitals, research institutions, and governments is crucial for ongoing progress.

Questions

  1. What strategies are mentioned to manage chronic diseases?
  2. Why is collaboration important in medical research?
  3. Give examples of preventive care measures.

Words in Context Tips

  • Chronic → long-lasting or persistent.
  • Preventive → intended to stop something before it happens.
  • Emphasized → highlighted or stressed.
  • Collaboration → working together.
  • Complications → difficulties or problems.

Tip: Look for definitions and clues within the same sentence or paragraph.

Common Errors

Unique Mistake: Neglecting Context of Numbers & Data

Many questions include quantitative info; ignoring units, timing, or comparisons can lead to wrong answers.
