Mistake #16: Misusing Proportions / Ratios

Section: Math

Why students pick it

They confuse numerators and denominators or swap values incorrectly.

Why it’s wrong

Incorrect setup produces wrong answers even if arithmetic is correct.

What the SAT is testing

Ratio understanding and correct setup.

How to avoid it

Set up ratios carefully and double-check which quantity corresponds to numerator vs denominator.

Try it yourself!

If 3 pencils cost $2, how much do 9 pencils cost?
