Mistake #6: Picking the Most Familiar Word

Section: Writing

Why students pick it

They recognize a word from vocabulary study and assume it is correct.

Why it’s wrong

The SAT context may require a specific nuance; a familiar word may be partially correct but not the best fit.

What the SAT is testing

Precision and understanding context.

How to avoid it

Always read the sentence context carefully and choose the word that fully fits meaning.
